Suppliers We Love To Work With!

Localitty's soul mission is to supply local produce from within 50 miles (location dependent) of your house to you. We believe that having everything in one place to choose from will encourage more people to support local farmers and vendors.

With us doing the hard work of finding that local butcher, green grocer or fish monger and bringing everything to your door with next day delivery available to you.

Although we are currently only operating in the Essex area, we are always on the search for new suppliers in different regions to work with to expand our reach. So if you are a local provider, please reach out to us and we will get back to you as soon as we can.
